Research

My recent time is being spent in learning tools and techniques from algebraic and differential topology that can help solve problems in algebraic geometry. In particular, I am interested in the connection between motivic homotopy theory and affine fibration theory. In a broader perspective, this also includes various cohomology theories and birational geometry.
